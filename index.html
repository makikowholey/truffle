Truffle Shuffle: Major Shakeups in the Truffle Export Industry

By Makiko Wholey

We tend to think of truffles in America as an imported French delicacy, and from 1979 to 2000 France reigned as the biggest fresh truffle exporters in the world. As of 2010 France ranks the lowest in terms of value of truffle exports to the U.S., with a stated value of zero. Countries like Ukraine, Hungry, and China are taking over the U.S. export market from Europe.
